Attractions I'd Skip

  • Hyde Park – While it’s central and historic, it feels more like a regular city park. Instead: Take a stroll through Greenwich Park, which offers expansive greenery and stunning panoramic views of London from the Royal Observatory.
  • Riding the London Eye – Expensive tickets and long queues for a ride that’s over too quickly. Instead: Visit the Sky Garden — it's completely free (with advance booking), has lush indoor gardens, and offers breathtaking skyline views from a glass dome.
  • Buckingham Palace – Most visitors just view it from outside the gates, which can feel underwhelming. Instead: Catch the Changing of the Guard for a more active experience or visit nearby St. James’s Park for a more scenic and relaxing atmosphere.
